Privacy Policy - English

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Below is how we handle your information across our different services:

Sr. No. DOI Website DOI Chatbot Hotline Treatment Process
1 No personal/sensitive information is collected User is encouraged not to disclose any identifying information "When someone calls, the DOI team cannot see the phone number" Clients are referred into treatment using the PIN provided
2 We do not collect IP addresses We do not store any IP address or mac address on our service Caller is encouraged not to disclose any identifying information unless caller proactively shares it Throughout the treatment, clients are addressed only by their PIN.
3 No information is saved when chatbot is offline/online Caller is provided with Personal Identification Number (PIN) for further communication All client files are documented using PIN only
4 Any referral to therapy from the chatbot is conducted via secure, encrypted email service only and protect all confidential communication. Access to any treatment-related documents is strictly limited.
